About DeskKit

DeskKit is built by an Android and Flutter developer based in Kerala who got tired of "Top 10" desk-gear posts written by people who clearly never sat at a real desk trying to get work done. Coding all day means the desk setup isn't decoration — it's the difference between a productive session and a sore back by evening. That's the lens every review here is written through.

How picks are made

Each category starts from a shortlist of products that actually show up in search and on Amazon's bestseller lists for that category. They're compared on the things that matter for the stated use case — for a laptop stand: height range, stability under typing pressure, weight, and price — and ranked accordingly. Reviews from verified buyers are read carefully to separate a genuine pattern from a single person's bad luck, and personal opinion is kept separate from the spec comparison.
